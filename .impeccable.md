## Design Context

### Users
- Home karaoke hosts and guests.
- Primary contexts are mobile queueing/settings and large-screen playback or stage display.
- The main job is to run a social karaoke session smoothly with minimal friction and clear state.

### Brand Personality
- Personality: clear, dependable, modern, slightly playful.
- Emotional goal: hosts should feel in control during live sessions and guests should feel the stage is polished and intentional.
- Tone should favor direct feedback, calm confidence, and practical delight over decoration.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Keep the dark, high-contrast karaoke aesthetic already established in the app.
- Favor presentation-first layouts for stage playback and compact, task-focused layouts for queue/settings.
- Use expressive but controlled accents; avoid visual noise, excessive glow, and anything that competes with the video or lyrics.

### Design Principles
1. Preserve the performance: the video/lyrics area should dominate playback surfaces.
2. Separate presentation from management: stage views should feel immersive, queue views should feel efficient.
3. Make state obvious: active, paused, next, and error states must be easy to read at a glance.
4. Keep controls intentional: fewer controls, clearer hierarchy, larger tap targets.
5. Use motion and accent only when they improve comprehension or flow.
